# Wishlist Feature

## Overview
The Wishlist feature allows customers to save products they are interested in for future reference. This is beneficial for users who want to keep track of items they may want to purchase later without adding them to their cart immediately.

## Key Features
- **Add to Wishlist**: Customers can add items to their wishlist from product pages.
- **View Wishlist**: Customers can view their wishlist at any time from their account dashboard.
- **Remove from Wishlist**: Customers can remove items from their wishlist if they no longer wish to keep them.
- **Share Wishlist**: Customers can share their wishlist with friends and family to showcase items they desire.

## User Benefits
- Saves time for future purchases.
- Helps in planning purchases efficiently.
- Enables sharing of gift ideas with others.
