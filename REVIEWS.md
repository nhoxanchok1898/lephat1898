# Product Reviews and Ratings System

## Overview
This document outlines the product reviews and ratings system implemented in our application. The system allows users to provide feedback on products they have purchased, which helps other customers make informed decisions.

## Features
- **User Reviews:** Customers can submit written reviews of products, sharing their experiences and opinions.
- **Rating System:** Each product can be rated on a scale of 1 to 5 stars, where 1 star indicates poor quality and 5 stars indicate excellent quality.
- **Sorting and Filtering:** Users can sort and filter reviews based on the rating, date, and helpfulness.
- **Verification:** Reviews can be marked as verified if the user has purchased the product, ensuring authenticity.

## How It Works
1. **Submitting a Review:** Users can submit their reviews through the product page. They need to provide a rating and an optional comment.
2. **Displaying Reviews:** Reviews are displayed on the product page, showing the average rating, individual reviews, and a breakdown of ratings.
3. **Moderation:** Reviews are moderated to prevent spam and inappropriate content.

## Benefits
- Helps build trust with potential customers.
- Provides valuable feedback to improve products and services.
- Encourages user engagement and interaction with the product page.

## Conclusion
An efficient reviews and ratings system is crucial for enhancing the shopping experience and increasing customer satisfaction. It plays a vital role in our product strategy, helping us understand customer preferences and improvements needed.
