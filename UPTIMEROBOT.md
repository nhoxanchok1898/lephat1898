# UptimeRobot Monitoring Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create an Account
1. Go to [UptimeRobot](https://uptimerobot.com/).
2. Click on the 'Sign Up' button.
3. Fill in your details or sign up using your Google account.
4. Verify your email address to complete the registration.

## Step 2: Add a Monitor
1. Log in to your UptimeRobot account.
2. Click on the 'Monitors' tab in the dashboard.
3. Click the '+ Add Monitor' button.
4. Choose a monitor type:
   - `HTTP(s)`: For website monitoring.
   - `Ping`: For monitoring server uptime.
   - `TCP`: For port monitoring.
   - `Keyword`: For checking specific keywords on a webpage.
5. Fill in the required fields (name, URL/IP, etc.) for the monitor type.
6. Set a monitoring interval (default is every 5 minutes).
7. Click 'Create Monitor' to finish.

## Step 3: Configure Alerts
1. Go to the 'My Settings' tab.
2. Click on 'Alert Contacts' in the sidebar.
3. Click the '+ Add Alert Contact' button.
4. Choose your preferred alert method (Email, SMS, etc.).
5. Fill in the required information and save.
6. Go back to the 'Monitors' tab, select a monitor, and click 'Edit Monitor.'  
7. Scroll down to the 'Alert Contacts' section and select the alert contacts you've created.
8. Save the changes.

## Step 4: Configure Monitoring Intervals
- The default monitoring interval is set to 5 minutes. You can adjust this when adding or editing a monitor.
- Choose intervals suited to your needs for real-time tracking or less frequent checks to save resources.

## Conclusion
Now, you are all set up with UptimeRobot! You can monitor your website or service's uptime efficiently and receive alerts in case of outages.
